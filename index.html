Social Media Trends Shift With Increased Screen Time

Tatiana Pinheiro

Oct 2, 2023

The rise and decline of social media usage amongst U.S teens ages 13-17 varies from platform to platform. From 2015 to 2022 Facebook has seen the most obvious decline while instagram and Tik Tok numbers have sky rocketed, making the two the most popular platforms on the market. But just how much time are teens spending on these applications?

Facebook

Tik Tok

Tik Tok has a 67% increase in its usage since it was introduced to the U.S market in 2018.

Screen Time

In 2015 teens averaged 6 hours and 40 minutes of screen time, while in 2021 the average time increased to 8 hours and 39 minutes, a 29.75 % increase.
